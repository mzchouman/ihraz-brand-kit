How It Works

1. Editors (Section 01): Click a card to open the interactive editor. Choose your variant, fill in the form, export as PDF.

2. Downloads (Section 02): Click any asset card to download the file directly. PPTX templates open in PowerPoint/Google Slides.

3. Reference (Section 03): Open brand guides for specifications and rules. Share these links with partners.

No software installation required. Everything runs in your browser.

Brand Consistency

All templates and assets use the official IHRAZ brand system: the Convergence Mark, emerald color palette (#10B981), Syne + Space Grotesk + Cairo typography, and bilingual (EN/AR) elements.

The designs are locked to ensure every piece of collateral your team produces is on-brand. For any custom requests, refer to the Brand Guidelines.
